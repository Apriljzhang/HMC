About Prince Education

A community of commitment. A school of excellence. Students of the future.

Prince Education is an international education services brand offering quality language programmes and professional development for learners of all ages and for corporate clients.

Who We Are

Prince Education operates under Hong Kong-Macau International Education Research Institute Limited. Over the past years, we have served more than 10,000 learners and have been committed to supporting international education across the Hong Kong-Macau Greater Bay Area.

Our teaching team comprises experienced educators, many with overseas qualifications and classroom experience. We invest heavily in curriculum design, learner experience, and long-term student development - not only short-term test outcomes.

Programmes combine face-to-face instruction, live online teaching, and our proprietary learning app so students can study flexibly while receiving consistent academic support and progress tracking.

Our Philosophy

We care about every learner's journey, efficiency, and personal growth. Our mission is to:

  • Deliver excellent instruction that supports admission to leading universities worldwide
  • Provide attentive, family-oriented service that connects students, parents, and schools
  • Ground learning in strong cultural foundations while cultivating global citizens and lifelong learners

Empowering and inspiring all students to excel as lifelong learners.

Academic platform and recognition

  • Academic pathway focus: We build learning and advising pathways to help learners progress towards leading universities worldwide, and we support institutions with international programme development.
  • IELTS partnership: Official IELTS partner organisation (code: TS00386).
  • Awards and memberships: Recognised by industry organisations and media, including features by Guangdong Radio and Television (Modern Education segment) and related honours.

Company Milestones

  • 2014 Prince Education incorporated in Singapore; Singapore teaching centre opened; adult workplace programmes launched (including government and corporate training).
  • 2018 Zhuhai teaching centre established; competency-based English programmes and corporate training for major employers including Flex Zhuhai.
  • 2020 Centre-wide upgrade of programmes and services; international MBA pathway support added to strengthen career competitiveness.
  • 2021 Hengqin port-area campus opened; enhanced services for Macau-based students and Greater Bay Area professionals.

Teaching & Learning

We operate a weekly scheduling system with flexible booking and leave management. All enrolled students access rich digital resources through the Prince Education app, with seamless switching between online and on-campus modes.

Small-group classes, full-time faculty for live online delivery, pre- and post-exam review, and staged assessments ensure effective feedback for students and teachers alike.
