Course Programmes

Comprehensive English and professional development pathways for academic, career, and corporate goals.

Programmes are delivered in small groups with full-time faculty, in English-medium classrooms designed to reflect authentic international learning environments. Contact us for schedules, placement, and pricing.

Competency & General English

General English

Integrated skills for daily, academic, and social communication; suitable for foundation through advanced levels.

Business English

Workplace communication, meetings, presentations, and professional correspondence (aligned with BEC competencies).

English for Interviews

Structured preparation for university admissions and job interviews, including mock interviews and feedback.

English & American Literature

Critical reading and analytical writing through selected literary texts for advanced learners.

Customized Corporate Training

Tailored syllabi for enterprises; on-site or at our centres. Past clients include major manufacturers in Zhuhai.

Examination & Certification Preparation

  • IELTS - Academic and General Training modules
  • TOEFL iBT - Integrated skills and test strategies
  • TOEIC - Workplace English assessment preparation
  • GRE / GMAT - Verbal and analytical writing support
  • CET-4 / CET-6 - National college English tests (China)
