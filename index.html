Welcome to Prince Education

Founded in 2014 and expanded across Zhuhai, Hengqin, Hong Kong, and Shanghai, Prince Education is built by experienced international educators. We focus on learner experience, long-term growth, and rigorous course design - supported by our proprietary learning app and interactive classrooms.

Our graduates and clients have progressed to leading universities in North America, the United Kingdom, Canada, Singapore, Japan, South Korea, Hong Kong, and Macau, and have strengthened their professional profiles through workplace English and executive programmes.
