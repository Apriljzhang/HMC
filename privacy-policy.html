Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Hong Kong-Macau International Education Research Institute Limited (the \"Company\", \"we\", \"us\") operating under the Prince Education brand collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our website and educational services.

1. Who we are

We are an international education services provider operating teaching centres in the Greater Bay Area, Hong Kong, and Shanghai. See our contact page for centre addresses.

2. Information we collect

  • Contact and enrolment data: name, email, phone, affiliation, course interests, and payment-related information when you enrol in our programmes
  • Website usage: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and cookies or similar technologies if analytics are enabled
  • Communications: content of emails or messages you send us

If you choose AALA conference registration from our Products & Services page, you will complete registration and payment on aalaconference.com. Data on that site is collected by the conference organisers under their own privacy practices. We do not control that website.

3. How we use information

  • Provide courses, advising, and customer support
  • Process enrolments and payments for our training services
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Send service-related notices (you may opt out of marketing where applicable)

4. Sharing of information

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with:

  • Payment processors and banks to complete transactions you authorize
  • Service providers (hosting, email) under confidentiality obligations
  • Authorities when required by law

5. Data retention

We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above, including statutory record-keeping periods for financial transactions.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data. No internet transmission is fully secure; use caution when sending sensitive information.

7. Your rights

Depending on applicable law, you may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your personal data by contacting us in writing at our teaching centre addresses listed on the contact page.

8. Cookies

Our site may use essential cookies for functionality. If we add analytics cookies, we will update this policy and, where required, obtain consent.

9. Children

Our services may involve minors enrolled with parental consent. Parents or guardians may contact us regarding a minor's data.

10. Changes

We may update this policy; the \"Last updated\" date will change. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
