AALA 2026 International Conference (collaboration)

The 12th International Conference of the Asian Association for Language Assessment (AALA)

  • Dates: 18-20 September 2026
  • Venue: City University of Macau, Macau SAR
  • Theme: Humanistic Approach to Assessment, Standards, Innovation, and Accountability
  • Audience: Researchers, educators, graduate students, and assessment professionals

Prince Education is a collaborator for AALA 2026. Registration information is collected on the AALA conference website; credit card payment is completed on this Prince Education website through our secure bank payment gateway.

Register on AALA (information) Pay on Prince Education

How to register and pay

  1. Complete your registration details on aalaconference.com/registration.html.
  2. When you choose credit card payment, you will be directed to Prince Education checkout on this website.
  3. Confirm your name, affiliation, fee category amount, and click Pay via bank gateway.
  4. Alternatively, add the registration to your cart here and proceed to checkout.
