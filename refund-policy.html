Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 2 June 2026

1. Training and service fees

  • Before service starts: Written cancellation requests may qualify for partial refund according to signed agreement terms.
  • After service starts: Completed sessions are non-refundable unless otherwise stated in writing.
  • Materials and third-party fees: Non-refundable once confirmed or issued.

2. Online card payments

Online card payments are processed through our secure bank payment gateway. If payment fails or is interrupted, please retry or contact us before requesting a chargeback.

3. Processing timeline

Approved refunds are normally returned to the original payment method within 14-30 business days.
